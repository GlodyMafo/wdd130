Overview

Purpose

The goal is to create an online presence for my rafting business to attract new customers, simplify bookings, provide relevant information and build an online community around my brand.

Audience

The website of my rafting company targets adventure enthusiasts, vacationing families, corporate teams seeking to strengthen cohesion, and travelers in search of unique experiences. Their desire is to have unforgettable adventures while feeling safe, all at a reasonable price. When visiting the site, they expect to find details about various rafting options, pricing, testimonials from previous customers, as well as information about locations and booking possibilities. Their unmet needs include clear information about safety measures and personalized recommendations. Typically, they access the site via mobile devices for convenience on the go, but optimization for desktop and laptop computers remains important for those planning trips from home or their workplace.
