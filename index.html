Republic Democratic of Congo

The DRC is the second largest country in Africa after Algeria, with Kinshasa as its capital. Its vegetation consists of equatorial forests and marshes. The Congo River basin has a dense hydrographic network and large floodplains. The official language of the DRC is French but the population of Congo speaks between 200 and more than 400 languages ​​depending on the classification12. Linguistically, it is one of the most multilingual countries in all of Africa. Congo has a very rich musical culture, its music known as rumba makes all of Africa dance. the DRC has on its territory the third highest peak in Africa, covered in eternal snow, named Pic Marguerite.
